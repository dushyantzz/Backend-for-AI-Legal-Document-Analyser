RENTAL AGREEMENT

This Rental Agreement is made on January 1, 2024, between John Smith (Landlord) and Jane Doe (Tenant) for the rental of property located at 123 Main Street, Anytown, USA.

TERMS AND CONDITIONS:

1. RENTAL PERIOD: The rental period shall be from January 1, 2024, to December 31, 2024.

2. RENT: The monthly rent shall be $1,500, payable on the first day of each month.

3. SECURITY DEPOSIT: Tenant shall pay a security deposit of $1,500 upon signing this agreement.

4. UTILITIES: Tenant is responsible for electricity, gas, water, and internet services.

5. MAINTENANCE: Landlord is responsible for major repairs and maintenance of the property.

6. PETS: No pets are allowed without written permission from the Landlord.

7. TERMINATION: Either party may terminate this agreement with 30 days written notice.

This agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of California.

Landlord: John Smith
Date: January 1, 2024
